View Campsites by Geographical Location

This feature allows users to look at campsites based on geographical location. The benefits of this feature are that users can choose campsites close to where they are or they choose locations near geographical features that they find interesting, and users can see all of the campsites at once. The cost of this feature is that users cannot filter campsites by their interests.

How to use this feature: Look at the red markers on the map. If the location of a marker interests you click on the marker to reveal the name of the campsite and more details about it.
